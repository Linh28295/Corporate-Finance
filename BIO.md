# Bio — Nguyen Bui Ngoc Linh

Nguyen Bui Ngoc Linh is a Senior Manager of Operations & Technology Strategy and Transformation at Prudential Vietnam, where she leads enterprise-wide change across a multi-million dollar transformation portfolio. With eight years of progressive experience across life insurance, retail, and financial services in Vietnam, she specializes in corporate strategy, process re-engineering, and operating model design.

Her expertise spans portfolio governance, change management, Target Operating Model (TOM) design, and financial stewardship. Key achievements include delivering **20% cost optimization** through process re-engineering and operating model refinement, and receiving Prudential's **Alexandrite Award** for her pivotal contributions to the Health Strategy and Claims Business Process Re-engineering initiative.

Linh holds a Bachelor of Commerce in Marketing and Management from Curtin Singapore and is pursuing an Executive MBA at the University of Hawaiʻi at Mānoa's Shidler College of Business, with a focus on corporate finance, valuation, and enterprise leadership. She is PROSCI-certified in change management and proficient in Celonis process mining, advanced financial modeling, and data-driven performance frameworks.

Her long-term goal is to shape the future of financial services in Southeast Asia by integrating technology-driven strategy with sustainable, people-centered operating models.
