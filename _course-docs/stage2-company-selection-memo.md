# Stage 2: Company Selection Memo

**Weight:** 10% of project score (70% deliverable / 30% presentation)
**Deliverable:** Markdown memo (`.md`) + in-class presentation

---

## Overview

Write an executive memo selecting and justifying the company you will analyze for the remainder of this project.

## Deliverable

A 400–600 word Markdown memo saved to `docs/decisions/` with filename:
`YYYY-MM-DD-{lastname}-{company-slug}-selection.md`

## Required sections

1. **Company Overview** — Name, ticker, exchange, industry, market cap, reporting currency
2. **Selection Rationale** — Why this company?
3. **Data Availability & Sources** — Confirm access to audited financials
4. **Preliminary Observations** — 2–3 initial hypotheses
5. **Ratio Categories Preview** — Which categories are most relevant
6. **Data Collection Plan** — Statements needed, currency/standard considerations

## Rubric

| Criterion | % of deliverable |
|-----------|-----------------:|
| Company Selection & Rationale | 25% |
| Analytical Framing & Hypotheses | 25% |
| Data Source Identification | 25% |
| Professionalism & Communication | 25% |
