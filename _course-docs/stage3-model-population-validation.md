# Stage 3: Populated Financials

**Weight:** 20% of project score
**Format:** Upload-only — no presentation component
**Deliverable:** Populated `.xlsx` with company financials

---

## Overview

Populate the provided ratios template with real financial data for the company you selected at Stage 2. Income Statement, Balance Sheet, and Cash Flow only.

## Deliverable

Saved to `models/builds/` as `YYYY-MM-DD-{company-slug}-financials.xlsx`

## Workbook requirements

- Income Statement, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow: current year + prior year
- Market/analyst assumptions entered
- Cover & Instructions tab: reporting standard, currency, fiscal year end, source URL

## Rubric

| Criterion | % |
|-----------|---|
| Data accuracy (ties to source 10-K) | 40% |
| Completeness (both years populated) | 25% |
| Source documentation | 20% |
| Auto-computed ratios resolve cleanly | 15% |
